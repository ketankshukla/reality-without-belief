# Book 3 Cover Image Prompts

## Reality Without Belief: The Theater of Conviction

### How Culture, Identity, and Outrage Replace Thinking

All prompts are for 9:16 portrait orientation book covers.

## Prompt 1: Seeing Through the Theater

A stunning young white woman in her early 20s standing calm and centered in the middle of a chaotic scene. Around her, blurred figures are shouting, pointing, holding signs, engaged in heated argument — representing the theater of outrage and conviction. She alone is still, composed, with an expression of clear-eyed awareness and quiet power. The chaos swirls around her but does not touch her. She sees through it all. The color palette contrasts: the chaotic figures in harsh, saturated reds and blues (tribal colors), while she is bathed in warm, natural golden light. Photorealistic, dramatic depth of field with her in sharp focus and the chaos softly blurred. Cover dimensions: 2700 x 4800 pixels (9:16 portrait). Text layout from top to bottom — Title: "REALITY WITHOUT BELIEF" in uppercase Garamond Bold, approximately 180pt, centered horizontally, positioned 8% from the top edge, white or warm cream color with subtle drop shadow for legibility. Book number: "BOOK III" in uppercase Garamond Regular, approximately 90pt, centered horizontally, positioned 14% from the top edge, with wide letter spacing (tracking 200), same color as title. Subtitle: "How Culture, Identity, and Outrage Replace Thinking" in Garamond Italic, approximately 64pt, centered horizontally, positioned 19% from the top edge, same color as title. Author name: "KETAN SHUKLA" in uppercase Garamond Bold, approximately 120pt, centered horizontally, positioned 90% from the top edge (near the bottom), with generous letter spacing (tracking 250), same color as title. All text must be clearly legible against the background image.
