# Book 4 Cover Image Prompts
## Reality Without Belief: Living from What Is So
### How to See, Listen, Decide, and Respond Without Belief

All prompts are for 9:16 portrait orientation book covers.

## Prompt 1: The Four Senses

A beautiful young white woman in her early 20s in a powerful, centered stance with her eyes open and engaged. She is depicted in four overlapping, slightly transparent layers — seeing (eyes sharp and clear), listening (head slightly tilted, fully attentive), deciding (hand raised in a gesture of choice), and responding (body in motion, stepping forward). The four layers merge into one unified, present, powerful figure. The background is a clean, warm gradient from deep navy at top to golden light at bottom, representing the transition from confusion to clarity. Her expression radiates calm confidence and full engagement with reality. Photorealistic, artistic composite with elegant transparency effects. At the top of the cover in large, elegant Garamond font, centered: "REALITY WITHOUT BELIEF". Below it, in smaller but clearly legible Garamond font, properly spaced: "How to See, Listen, Decide, and Respond Without Belief". At the bottom of the cover, in large centered Garamond font with generous letter spacing: "KETAN SHUKLA".

## Prompt 5: The Still Point

A dramatic image of a beautiful young white woman in her early 20s standing perfectly still and grounded at the center of a dynamic, moving world. Around her, elements of daily life are in motion — people walking, traffic flowing, screens flickering, conversations happening — all captured in artistic motion blur. She alone is perfectly sharp, perfectly still, perfectly present. She is the still point in the turning world — the person who sees, listens, decides, and responds from reality rather than from the blur of belief and reactivity. Her expression is one of profound clarity and empowered calm. The contrast between her stillness and the world's movement is the visual story.
