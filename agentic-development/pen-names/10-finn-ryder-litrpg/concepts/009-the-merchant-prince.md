# The Merchant Prince

## Author
**Finn Ryder** — LitRPG

---

## Genre
LitRPG / Economic Progression / Trade Empire

---

## Concept

When the System integrated with Earth, most people focused on combat classes. Warriors, mages, rogues — the flashy paths to power. Victor Chen chose Merchant. Not because he couldn't fight. Because he understood that in any system, the people who control resources control everything.

The Merchant class starts weak. No combat abilities, minimal stats, a few trade skills. Most players who choose it abandon it within a week. Victor doubles down. He grinds Trade, Negotiation, Appraisal, and Economics while everyone else grinds swords.

By month three, Victor controls the market for health potions in three zones. By month six, he's the exclusive supplier of enchanting materials to the top guilds. By year one, no major raid happens without equipment that passed through his trading network.

The combat players call him a parasite. They don't understand: Victor isn't playing the same game. They're fighting monsters. He's building an empire. And when a world boss appears that requires resources no single guild can afford, everyone comes to the Merchant Prince.
