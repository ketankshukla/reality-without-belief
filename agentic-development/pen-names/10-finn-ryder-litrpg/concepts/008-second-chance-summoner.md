# Second Chance Summoner

## Author

---

## Genre
LitRPG / Time Loop / Summoner Class

---

## Concept

Marcus died at level 99, one boss away from reaching the level cap. The Demon King's final attack killed him, killed his party, killed his summons. Game over. World lost. Apocalypse complete.

Then he woke up at level 1, three years earlier, memories intact.

Marcus knows exactly how the apocalypse unfolds. He knows which dungeons spawn when, which monsters drop which materials, which players become heroes and which become villains. He has three years to reach the Demon King again — and this time, win.

His class is Summoner, a support role that most players consider underpowered. Marcus knows better. In his first timeline, he discovered too late that Summoner has the highest theoretical ceiling in the game. The creatures you summon level independently. Their skills evolve. And there's no limit to how many you can have active — just to how many you can control.

Last time, Marcus reached the Demon King with five summons at level 90. This time, he's going to reach the Demon King with an army.
