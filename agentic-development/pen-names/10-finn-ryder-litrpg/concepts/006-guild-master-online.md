# Guild Master Online

## Author
**Finn Ryder** — LitRPG

---

## Genre
LitRPG / Guild Management / Strategy Progression

---

## Concept

Ryan Park was never a great player. Average reflexes, mediocre builds, nothing special. What he was great at was organizing. Scheduling. Making sure thirty people showed up at the same time with the right gear. In every MMO he played, he ended up as guild master — not because he was the strongest, but because someone had to do the paperwork.

When Exodus Online launches with full immersion VR and real-money stakes, Ryan starts as just another player. Within a month, he's running the fastest-growing guild on the server. Not because he can clear content — because he can manage the people who do.

Guild Master is a hidden class. It doesn't show up in character creation. You unlock it by doing the work: recruiting, organizing, resolving conflicts, building systems. When Ryan's class evolves, he gains abilities no one has ever seen: buffs that scale with guild size, skills that improve based on member satisfaction, ultimate abilities tied to guild achievements.

The top guilds notice. They've been fighting over territory, competing for world firsts, locked in eternal rivalry. Now there's a new player who doesn't compete directly — he builds organizations that outperform everyone else's. And he's figured out that the real endgame isn't dungeons or PvP. It's guild wars.
