# The Baking Contest Murder

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Baking Competition Mystery

---

## Concept

The Great Thornbury Bake-Off is the village's most anticipated event — amateur bakers competing for the Golden Whisk trophy and the admiration of the entire county. When judge Marjorie Finch-Hatton collapses face-first into a Victoria sponge, everyone assumes a heart attack. When the autopsy reveals cyanide in her afternoon tea, everyone becomes a suspect.

Baker and café owner Sophie Turner was Marjorie's goddaughter — and the only person who knew that Marjorie had been receiving threatening letters for months. She'd dismissed them as crank mail, but now Sophie realizes someone was serious. Very serious.

The suspects read like a recipe for disaster: the baker Marjorie destroyed with a scathing review, the rival judge she'd publicly humiliated, her own nephew who stood to inherit, and the competition organizer whose embezzlement Marjorie had discovered.

Sophie investigates between batches of scones, using the baking competition as cover to interview suspects. The killer is someone who knows their way around a kitchen — and poison.
