# The Antique Shop Affair

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Antique Mystery

---

## Concept

Cordelia "Delia" Whitmore runs Whitmore's Antiques in the market town of Hartwell Green. She specializes in Georgian silverware, can date a piece by its hallmarks from across the room, and has a keen eye for forgeries. When a customer is found dead in her shop, clutching a silver candlestick that shouldn't exist, Delia's expertise becomes crucial to solving the murder.

The victim, Charles Pemberton, was a collector — and, Delia discovers, a fence for stolen antiques. The candlestick in his hand was part of a set that was stolen from Ashworth Manor forty years ago, a theft that was never solved and that someone has been trying to keep buried ever since.

As Delia investigates, she uncovers a web connecting the local aristocracy, the antiques trade, and a decades-old crime that someone is still willing to kill to protect. The murder isn't about the candlestick — it's about what the candlestick proves.
