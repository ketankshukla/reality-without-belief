# The Pub Quiz Puzzle

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Pub Mystery

---

## Concept

The weekly pub quiz at The Crow's Nest has run for twenty years — same night, same tables, same fierce rivalries. When quizmaster Walter Graves is found dead in the snug, the question isn't who had motive. It's who didn't.

Walter was the most unpopular man in Millbury. He'd cheated half the village in business deals, spread vicious gossip, and used his position as quizmaster to humiliate anyone he disliked. The pub regulars are openly relieved he's dead. The police are overwhelmed with suspects.

Publican's daughter Nell Cooper grew up pulling pints and listening to village secrets. She knows everyone in the pub that night, knows their histories with Walter, knows things the police will never uncover through official channels. When the investigation stalls, she starts asking questions over drinks.

The murder, she discovers, was as clever as any pub quiz answer — hidden in plain sight, executed during the noise and confusion of a disputed question, using a method that required intimate knowledge of the pub's layout. The killer is someone who's sat at the same table every Thursday for years.
