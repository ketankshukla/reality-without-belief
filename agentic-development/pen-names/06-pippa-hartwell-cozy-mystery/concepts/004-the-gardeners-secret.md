# The Gardener's Secret

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Garden Mystery

---

## Concept

Iris Penrose has won the Bramblewick Garden Competition for fifteen consecutive years. Her cottage garden is legendary — a riot of color and scent that draws visitors from three counties. So when she's found dead among her prize-winning dahlias, the village is shocked. When the autopsy reveals poisoning by foxglove, a plant that grows in every garden in Bramblewick, the police are stumped.

Iris's neighbor, retired botanist Dr. Helena Marsh, knows something the police don't: Iris was terrified of foxglove. She'd pulled every specimen from her garden years ago. Someone had to have introduced it deliberately.

Helena begins investigating, using her botanical expertise to trace the source of the poison. The trail leads through the competitive underbelly of the garden show circuit — rivalries, sabotage, stolen cultivars, and resentments fertilized for decades.

The suspects include last year's runner-up (still bitter), the newcomer whose modern garden Iris criticized publicly, and Iris's own protégé, who stood to inherit her seed collection. Helena must dig through the soil of village secrets to find the root of the murder.
