# The Manor House Mystery

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Country House Mystery

---

## Concept

Ashworth Manor has been in the Ashworth family for four centuries. When Lord Ashworth dies under suspicious circumstances during the annual charity gala, his family immediately closes ranks — and housekeeper Mrs. Dorothy "Dot" Finch finds herself caught between her loyalty to the family and her certainty that someone in this house committed murder.

Dot has worked at Ashworth Manor for thirty years. She knows every secret passage, every family skeleton, every whispered conversation behind closed doors. She knows that Lord Ashworth was about to change his will, that his son owes gambling debts to dangerous people, that his daughter's marriage is a sham, and that his second wife was having an affair.

When the police investigation focuses on an innocent groundskeeper, Dot's conscience won't let her stay silent. But investigating the family that employs you is dangerous — especially when the murderer knows exactly how much you know.

2. **The Death** — Lord Ashworth dies

4. **The Secrets** — What Dot knows

7. **Dot Decides** — Beginning her own inquiry
8. **The Evidence** — What she finds
