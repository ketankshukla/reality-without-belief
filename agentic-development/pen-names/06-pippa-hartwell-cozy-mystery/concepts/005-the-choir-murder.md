# The Choir Murder

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Church Mystery

---

## Concept

The St. Michael's Church Choir has performed at the Cotswolds Choral Festival for forty years. This year, their performance ends with a death — lead tenor Reginald Blackstone collapses mid-hymn, and it's not the high C that killed him.

Choir director Eleanor Ashby, who's been managing musical egos and personal vendettas for two decades, finds herself conducting an investigation instead of a rehearsal. Someone in her choir is a murderer, and everyone had a reason to want Reginald gone.

Reginald was talented, insufferable, and knew secrets about everyone. The bass section leader he'd been blackmailing. The soprano who'd had an affair with him decades ago. The organist he'd publicly humiliated. The vicar whose embezzlement he'd discovered. Even Eleanor herself, whose husband had been Reginald's business partner in a deal that went very wrong.

As Eleanor investigates between rehearsals (the show must go on — the funeral is Tuesday), she discovers that Reginald's death was years in the planning. Someone in the choir has been waiting for exactly the right moment. And they've composed a murder as carefully as any symphony.
