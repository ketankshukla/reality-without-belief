# The Knitting Circle Mystery

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Craft Circle Mystery

---

## Concept

The Millbrook Knitting Circle has met every Wednesday for thirty years. Eight women, endless cups of tea, and gossip that could fill novels. When member Patricia Dawson is found strangled with one of her own hand-spun yarn skeins, the remaining seven realize that one of them is a murderer.

Newly widowed Frances Gray, the circle's quietest member, has spent decades watching and listening while her fingers worked the needles. She's observed every tension, noted every grudge, catalogued every whispered secret that the other members thought she didn't hear.

Now she uses that knowledge to investigate. Patricia wasn't just a member — she was the village blackmailer, collecting secrets like she collected rare wool. Someone finally decided to cut the thread.

The suspects include the retired teacher with a secret past, the vicar's wife whose marriage isn't what it seems, the wealthy widow whose fortune came from suspicious sources, and Frances's own sister-in-law, whose husband died under circumstances that Patricia had been asking questions about.

Frances must unravel the mystery before the next meeting — or risk being the next victim.

## Suggested Chapter Structure (10 chapters)

1. **The Circle** — Wednesday meeting, the women
2. **The Body** — Patricia found dead
3. **The Observer** — Frances's secret knowledge
4. **The Secrets** — What Patricia collected
5. **The Suspects** — Seven women, seven motives
6. **The Blackmail** — Understanding Patricia's power
7. **The Unraveling** — Frances investigates
8. **The Pattern** — Connecting the threads
9. **The Confrontation** — Naming the killer
10. **The Knitting Circle Mystery** — Resolution
