# The Bookshop Murder

## Author
**Pippa Hartwell** — Cozy Mystery

---

## Genre
Cozy Mystery / Amateur Sleuth / Bookshop Mystery

---

## Concept

Rosemary Flynn has run The Turning Page bookshop in Ashworth-on-Sea for thirty years. She knows every customer's taste, every local's secrets, and exactly which books to recommend for any mood. What she doesn't know is why someone would murder Geoffrey Blackwood, the reclusive author who came to her shop every Thursday for the past decade.

Geoffrey was found in the rare books room, struck over the head with a first edition he'd spent months trying to acquire. The irony isn't lost on Rosemary — or on the killer, who left the book perfectly positioned beside the body.

The police suspect robbery gone wrong, but nothing was taken. Rosemary suspects something more personal. Geoffrey was writing a memoir, one that would expose secrets from his years as a literary critic in London. Half the village has connections to that world, and Geoffrey's sharp tongue made enemies everywhere.

With her assistant (an aspiring mystery novelist who's delighted by the real-life plot) and her book club (four retired women who've read enough mysteries to have opinions), Rosemary begins to investigate. The clues are hidden in Geoffrey's final manuscript — which the killer is desperately trying to find before anyone can read it.
