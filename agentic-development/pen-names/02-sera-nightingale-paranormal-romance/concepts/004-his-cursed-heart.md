# His Cursed Heart

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Fae Romance / Curse Romance

---

## Concept

Lord Theron of the Winterhold has been cursed for three hundred years. A witch's dying revenge turned his heart to ice — literally. It beats, but slowly, and every year it freezes a little more. When it stops entirely, so does he. The curse can only be broken by a love freely given, but no one can love a monster whose touch brings frostbite.

Wren Calloway doesn't believe in curses. She's a practical herbalist who's come to the Winterhold estate to harvest rare plants that only grow in magically frozen soil. She expects cold. She expects isolation. She doesn't expect the devastatingly beautiful lord who watches her from the shadows, whose breath crystallizes in the air, whose eyes hold three centuries of loneliness.

She definitely doesn't expect to feel drawn to him.

Theron knows better than to hope. He's had curse-breakers before — women who came believing they could love him for the reward, only to discover that love isn't something you can force. Each failure cost him decades of heartbeat. He has perhaps ten years left. He should send the herbalist away.

But Wren doesn't want to break his curse. She doesn't even believe in it at first. She's just there for the plants. And somehow, that's what makes her different. She sees him as a person, not a problem to be solved. She argues with him, challenges him, makes him laugh for the first time in a century.

The problem is, the curse isn't just in his heart. It's spreading. And the closer Wren gets to him, the more danger she's in from the very cold she's learning to love.

---

## Key Themes
- Love as choice, not solution
- Three centuries of loneliness
- The danger of loving someone who could hurt you
- Curse-breaking that isn't about the curse

---

## Tone
Achingly romantic with genuine danger. The cold is real and lethal.

---

## Unique Angle
She doesn't come to break the curse — she comes for plants. The romance develops without agenda.

---

## Target Audience
Beauty and the Beast lovers, curse romance fans, readers who want slow-burn with stakes.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (12 chapters)

1. **The Winterhold** — Wren arrives, first sight of the frozen estate
2. **The Lord** — Meeting Theron, feeling the cold
3. **The Work** — Her research, his watching
4. **First Touch** — Accidental contact, the danger clear
5. **The Story** — Learning about the curse
6. **Growing Closer** — Conversations, connection
7. **The Spreading Cold** — Curse worsening, stakes rising
8. **Almost** — First almost-kiss, the danger
9. **The Previous Attempts** — Learning about others who tried
10. **The Choice** — Wren deciding to stay despite risk
11. **Love Freely Given** — The confession
12. **His Cursed Heart** — Resolution; HEA
