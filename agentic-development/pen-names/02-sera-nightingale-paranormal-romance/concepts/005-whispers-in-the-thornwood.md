# Whispers in the Thornwood

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Dark Fae Romance / Forbidden Desire

---

## Concept

The Thornwood gives you what you want most. That's what the village elders say, and that's why no one enters it. Because what you want most is rarely what you should have.

Elara Vance knows what she wants most: her mother back. It's been ten years since her mother walked into the Thornwood and never returned. Ten years of grief, of questions, of the village insisting that the forest simply "kept" her. Now Elara is old enough to go looking.

What she finds isn't her mother. It's Kieran, a fae lord bound to the Thornwood, cursed to grant desires he has no power to deny. He's the one who gave her mother what she wanted — and he's been waiting for Elara ever since. Because what her mother wanted most was for her daughter to be safe. And the only way Kieran could grant that wish was to bind Elara's fate to his own.

Now Elara has a choice. Stay in the Thornwood and be protected forever. Or leave and break her mother's last wish — freeing herself but unleashing something her mother saw coming ten years ago. Something her mother died to prevent.

Kieran can't tell her which choice is right. He can only offer her what she desires most. And the longer she stays, the more she realizes what she desires most might be him.

---

## Key Themes
- Desire vs. wisdom
- Parental sacrifice and its complications
- Forbidden attraction in trapped circumstances
- The cost of protection

---

## Tone
Darkly seductive, layered with mystery. The forest is alive with wanting.

---

## Unique Angle
The love interest can't lie but must grant desires — creating tension between what's wanted and what's wise.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love trapped-together romance, morally complex fae, and desire as magic system.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (11 chapters)

1. **The Edge of the Thornwood** — Elara entering, the warnings
2. **The Forest's Welcome** — Strangeness, beauty, danger
3. **The Bound Lord** — Meeting Kieran, learning his nature
4. **Her Mother's Wish** — The truth about what happened
5. **The Binding** — What it means for Elara
6. **Days in the Wood** — Living with Kieran, growing attraction
7. **What She Wants** — Desires surfacing, increasingly personal
8. **The Threat Outside** — What her mother foresaw
9. **The Temptation** — Giving in to attraction
10. **The Choice** — Stay protected or risk everything
11. **Whispers in the Thornwood** — Resolution; HEA
