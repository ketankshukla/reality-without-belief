# Bound by Silver and Shadow

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Shifter Romance / Enemies to Lovers

---

## Concept

The Ashford family has hunted wolves for seven generations. Not ordinary wolves — the Shadowborn, shifters who walk in human skin and wolf form, who've terrorized the northern territories since before the colonies. Maeve Ashford was raised with silver in her blood and hate in her heart.

Then she's attacked in the forest, left for dead, and saved by exactly the thing she was taught to kill.

Cade isn't just any Shadowborn. He's the Alpha's son, heir to the largest pack in the region, and he should have let the hunter's daughter die. But something about her called to the wolf in him — something that shouldn't exist between their kinds. He brings her to his den, heals her wounds, and waits for the inevitable moment when she tries to kill him.

She does try. Multiple times. But she's also trapped in Shadowborn territory in winter, with no weapons and no way home. To survive, she has to accept Cade's protection. To accept his protection, she has to question everything her family taught her about monsters.

As Maeve learns the truth about the Shadowborn — that they're not mindless killers but a persecuted people, that the Ashford family's "hunts" have been closer to genocide — she has to decide where her loyalty lies. Her family will never accept her choice. The pack may never trust a hunter's daughter. And the bond growing between her and Cade is forbidden by both their peoples.

But some bonds are stronger than silver, and some shadows hold more than darkness.

---

## Key Themes
- Inherited hatred vs. personal truth
- Romeo and Juliet with agency and survival
- Persecution and who gets called "monster"
- Bonds that defy expectation

---

## Tone
Tense enemies-to-lovers with genuine danger. The hate is real before the love is.

---

## Unique Angle
The "monster hunter's daughter falls for the monster" trope complicated by genuine atrocity on both sides.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love shifter romance with enemies-to-lovers, forbidden love, and found family.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (12 chapters)

1. **The Hunt** — Maeve in the forest, the attack
2. **Saved by the Enemy** — Cade's choice to save her
3. **Captive or Guest** — Trapped in the den, first encounters
4. **The First Attempt** — She tries to kill him
5. **Winter's Trap** — No escape until spring
6. **Learning the Pack** — Discovering they're not monsters
7. **The Truth About Ashford** — What her family really did
8. **Growing Closer** — Reluctant attraction becomes undeniable
9. **The Bond** — Something supernatural between them
10. **Her Family Comes** — The Ashfords arrive hunting
11. **Choice** — Maeve between two worlds
12. **Bound by Silver and Shadow** — Resolution; HEA
