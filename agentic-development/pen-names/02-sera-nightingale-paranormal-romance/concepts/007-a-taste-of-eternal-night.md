# A Taste of Eternal Night

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Vampire Romance / Dark Romance

---

## Concept

Sera Chen was supposed to be a quick meal. A mortal wandering too close to vampire territory, alone at night, practically an invitation. Instead, she became Lord Alexei Voronov's obsession — because when he tasted her blood, he tasted something that hasn't existed in five hundred years: sunlight.

Sera is a Dawnblood, one of the rare humans whose blood carries the memory of light. To vampires, her blood is intoxicating, addictive, and ultimately lethal — too much exposure to Dawnblood burns them from within. She's the most dangerous thing Alexei has ever wanted, and he wants her desperately.

He claims her instead of killing her, taking her to his court as a "guest" while he figures out what to do. Kill her and remove the temptation. Keep her and risk addiction. Or find a way to make her his — permanently — without destroying himself in the process.

Sera doesn't know what she is. She only knows that the terrifying vampire lord who kidnapped her looks at her like she's salvation and damnation wrapped in one body. She should be trying to escape. Instead, she's trying to understand why she feels more alive in his eternal night than she ever did in the day.

The problem is, other vampires have noticed her. Older ones. Hungrier ones. And they're willing to start a war to taste what Alexei has claimed.

---

## Key Themes
- Desire that's literally dangerous
- Power imbalance and shifting control
- What makes someone worth the risk
- Finding belonging in dark places

---

## Tone
Sensual and dangerous. The vampire world is seductive but genuinely threatening.

---

## Unique Angle
Her blood is addictive and lethal to him — the romance is literally playing with fire.

---

## Target Audience
Vampire romance readers who want heat, danger, and possessive love interests.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (12 chapters)

1. **The Hunt** — Alexei stalking prey, finding Sera
2. **The Taste** — The bite, the revelation
3. **Claimed** — Taken to his court
4. **The Court of Night** — Vampire politics, other predators
5. **Dangerous Proximity** — Living with him, growing tension
6. **The Addiction** — He can't stay away
7. **Others Notice** — Rivals, older vampires, threats
8. **Growing Feelings** — Not just blood anymore
9. **The War Begins** — Others come for her
10. **Choosing Darkness** — Sera's decision
11. **The Battle** — Defending what's his
12. **Eternal Night** — Resolution; HEA with transformation
