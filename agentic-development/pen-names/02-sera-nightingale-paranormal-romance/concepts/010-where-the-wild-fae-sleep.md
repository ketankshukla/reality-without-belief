# Where the Wild Fae Sleep

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Fae Romance / Quest Romance

---

## Concept

Every hundred years, the village of Thornhaven disappears. The buildings remain, but the people vanish — taken into the realm of the Wild Fae, a court so ancient and untamed that even other fae fear them. They return, eventually. Most of them. Changed.

This century, the village took Maura Blackwood's sister.

Maura refuses to wait a hundred years. She refuses to hope Lily will be among the returned. She's going into the Wild Court to bring her sister back — even though no one has ever entered voluntarily and returned with their mind intact.

The Wild Fae don't have a king or queen. They have the Dreaming Lord, Faelan, a being so old he's half-merged with the forest itself. To reach her sister, Maura needs his permission. To get his permission, she needs to interest him. The Wild Fae are easily bored and endlessly curious, and a mortal willing to walk into their realm rather than being taken... that's interesting.

Faelan agrees to guide her to her sister — in exchange for a story. One story for each mile traveled. And Maura must tell the truth, because the Wild Fae know when mortals lie.

As Maura travels deeper into the Wildwood, telling stories of her life, her hopes, her secrets, she realizes she's not just interesting Faelan. She's interesting herself to him. And the Dreaming Lord, for the first time in millennia, is waking up.

---

## Key Themes
- Stories as currency and connection
- The wildness within civilization
- Saving family vs. finding self
- Love that awakens something dormant

---

## Tone
Dreamlike but dangerous. The Wild Court is beautiful and wrong in ways that shift.

---

## Unique Angle
Payment in stories, not bargains. Truth as the cost of passage.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love mythic romance, quest narratives, and fae who are genuinely alien.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (12 chapters)

1. **The Vanishing** — Thornhaven disappears, Lily taken
2. **The Decision** — Maura choosing to go in
3. **The Dreaming Lord** — Meeting Faelan, the bargain
4. **First Story, First Mile** — Beginning the journey
5. **The Wildwood** — Beauty and strangeness
6. **Deeper Truths** — Stories becoming more personal
7. **Faelan Wakes** — His interest becoming something more
8. **The Heart of the Wild** — Approaching her sister's location
9. **The Price of Stories** — What truth costs
10. **Finding Lily** — Reunion, complication
11. **The Choice** — Leave with sister or stay with what's grown
12. **Where the Wild Fae Sleep** — Resolution; HEA
