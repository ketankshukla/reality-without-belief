# The Hunter's Dark Vow

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Enemies to Lovers / Supernatural Hunter Romance

---

## Concept

For a century, Lucian Voss has hunted her kind. The Order of the Silver Dawn trained him to kill demons, and he's very good at it. Efficient. Ruthless. Uncompromising. He has never failed to destroy his target.

Nyx has been hunted for just as long. She's a shadow demon — one of the rarest, most dangerous of her kind — and she's spent a hundred years running from men like Lucian. Not because she can't fight. Because she's tired of killing. She came to the mortal world to escape the demon courts, and every hunter who dies at her hands ties her closer to the violence she was trying to leave behind.

When Lucian finally corners her, she makes an unexpected choice: she surrenders. Not to die — to make a bargain. There's something in the mortal realm that's killing both humans and demons, something that's broken the rules both their peoples live by. Nyx knows what it is. Lucian has the skills to destroy it. Together, they might stop it before it rips a hole between worlds.

Lucian doesn't trust her. Everything he knows says demons lie, deceive, corrupt. But the evidence supports her story, and the threat is real. So he agrees to a temporary truce — and vows to kill her the moment the threat is ended.

What neither of them expects is that hunting together feels more right than hunting each other ever did.

---

## Key Themes
- Enemies forced to alliance
- Questioning everything you were taught
- Violence as trap vs. connection as escape
- Vows and their breaking

---

## Tone
Tense, adversarial sexual tension. Trust builds slowly through action.

---

## Unique Angle
Both are hunters. Both have killed the other's kind. The romance must overcome genuine mutual culpability.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love enemies-to-lovers with real enemies, supernatural hunters, and slow-burn built on conflict.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (12 chapters)

1. **The Hunt Ends** — Lucian corners Nyx
2. **The Surrender** — Her unexpected choice
3. **The Bargain** — Temporary alliance
4. **First Hunt Together** — Learning each other's methods
5. **The Enemy** — What's killing both their peoples
6. **Grudging Respect** — Competence forcing acknowledgment
7. **Histories** — Why they each became what they are
8. **The Shift** — Tension becoming attraction
9. **The Complication** — Others from both sides interfere
10. **The Truth** — What they actually feel
11. **The Final Hunt** — Facing the real threat together
12. **The Hunter's Dark Vow** — Resolution; HEA, vows broken and remade
