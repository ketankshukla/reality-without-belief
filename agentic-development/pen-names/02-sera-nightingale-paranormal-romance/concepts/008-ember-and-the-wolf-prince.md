# Ember and the Wolf Prince

## Author
**Sera Nightingale** — Paranormal Romance

---

## Genre
Paranormal Romance / Shifter Romance / Runaway Bride Romance

---

## Concept

Ember Ashworth is running from her wedding. Not cold feet — survival. Her father has sold her to a man three times her age, a lord whose previous wives died under suspicious circumstances. So she runs, in her wedding dress, into the one place no one will follow: the Wildlands.

The Wildlands belong to the wolf shifters, and they kill humans on sight. Ember knows this. She just figured a quick death was better than a slow one.

She didn't expect to be captured instead of killed. She definitely didn't expect to be brought before Prince Calder, alpha heir of the largest pack, who takes one look at the human bride in his territory and sees something that makes his wolf go very, very still.

Calder knows what she is. Ember doesn't. The mate bond — the supernatural recognition that ties a shifter to their destined partner — has just snapped into place. And it's chosen a human. A human who doesn't know about mates. A human whose family will come looking for her. A human who's currently staring at him like he's the next threat she needs to survive.

He can't let her go. The bond won't allow it. But he can't force her either — mates must choose freely. So he makes her an offer: stay in the Wildlands until her father stops searching. In exchange, she'll work off her keep.

Neither of them expects her to start feeling like home.

---

## Key Themes
- Escaping one cage into another kind of captivity
- Mate bonds and consent
- Finding safety in unexpected places
- The difference between ownership and belonging

---

## Tone
Tension between fear and attraction. Ember's survival instincts vs. growing feelings.

---

## Unique Angle
She's running from an arranged marriage into a supernatural bond — captivity to captivity, but fundamentally different.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love fated mates, runaway heroines, and protective alpha heroes.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (12 chapters)

1. **The Wedding** — Ember running, entering the Wildlands
2. **Captured** — Brought before the prince
3. **The Bond** — Calder's recognition, Ember's confusion
4. **The Bargain** — Stay and work, not a prisoner
5. **Pack Life** — Learning the shifters aren't monsters
6. **The Pursuit** — Her father's men searching
7. **Growing Closer** — Ember and Calder, tension building
8. **The Truth** — Learning about mate bonds
9. **Choosing** — Does she want this?
10. **The Attack** — Her father's forces find her
11. **The Stand** — Fighting for the right to choose
12. **Ember and the Wolf Prince** — Resolution; HEA
