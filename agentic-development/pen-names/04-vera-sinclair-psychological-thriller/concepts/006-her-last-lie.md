# Her Last Lie

## Author
**Vera Sinclair** — Psychological Thriller

---

## Genre
Psychological Thriller / Cold Case / Friendship Thriller

---

## Concept

Kate Morrison's best friend Lily vanished ten years ago. The case went cold. Everyone moved on. Kate didn't — she became a criminal psychologist, partly to understand how people disappear, partly because she couldn't let go.

Then the photographs start arriving.

They're from the night Lily disappeared — images Kate has never seen, showing angles of the party that prove someone else was there. Someone who was watching. Someone who documented everything and has kept silent for a decade.

But the photographs aren't from an unknown stalker. They're from inside the house. Taken from positions only someone at the party could have accessed. Someone Kate has known for ten years. Someone who's been her friend since the night Lily vanished.

As Kate analyzes the photographs — using every psychological technique she's learned — she realizes they're not just evidence. They're a confession. Someone in her circle wants her to figure it out. Someone who's been carrying this secret for ten years and has finally decided it's time for the truth to surface.

The question is why now. And whether the person revealing the truth is doing so to achieve justice — or to destroy Kate before she can discover what really happened that night.

---

## Key Themes
- Friendship and hidden guilt
- What we didn't see while we were looking
- The past's refusal to stay buried
- Being surrounded by the truth without seeing it

---

## Tone
Slow revelation, mounting dread. The truth was always in plain sight.

---

## Unique Angle
The evidence comes from inside the friend group. One of them has always known.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love friendship thrillers, cold case revelations, photographs as evidence.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (11 chapters)

1. **Ten Years Gone** — Kate's life now, the anniversary
2. **The First Photograph** — Arrival, confusion
3. **The Party** — Flashback to the night Lily vanished
4. **Analysis** — Kate examining the images
5. **More Photographs** — Additional evidence arriving
6. **The Angles** — Realizing they're from inside
7. **The Friends** — Examining who was where that night
8. **Confrontations** — Questioning those she trusts
9. **The Pattern** — Understanding what the sender wants
10. **The Truth** — What happened to Lily
11. **Her Last Lie** — Resolution
