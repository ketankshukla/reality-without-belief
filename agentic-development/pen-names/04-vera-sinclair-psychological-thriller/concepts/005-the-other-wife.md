# The Other Wife

## Author
**Vera Sinclair** — Psychological Thriller

---

## Genre
Psychological Thriller / Marriage Mystery / Identity Thriller

---

## Concept

When Jenna finds a second wedding ring in her husband's desk drawer, she expects the worst: another woman, another marriage, the cliché of the traveling businessman with two families. She prepares herself to confront him.

Then she reads the inscription inside the ring: "To Jenna, forever. — Michael"

The ring is addressed to her. The date engraved is four years before they met. And the ring is identical to the one she's wearing right now — the one Michael gave her at their wedding three years ago.

Michael has no explanation. He says he's never seen the ring before, that it must be some mistake, some mix-up. But Jenna starts digging, and what she finds is impossible: marriage records, photographs, a life that looks exactly like hers — with a woman who looks exactly like her — four years earlier.

Either Michael married her twice, or he married someone who could have been her twin. Either he's lying about not remembering, or someone has gone to extraordinary lengths to create a false paper trail.

Jenna needs to find the other wife. The one in the photographs. The one who signed the marriage certificate. The one who, according to every record Jenna can find, simply stopped existing three years ago — right around the time Jenna entered Michael's life.

## Key Themes
- Identity and doubles
- What husbands don't tell us
- The woman who came before
- How well do we know anyone?

---

## Tone
Identity paranoia. The mystery of the self preceding you.

---

## Unique Angle
The other woman isn't a rival — she might be a previous version of the protagonist herself.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love identity thrillers, marriage mysteries, doppelgänger suspense.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (11 chapters)

1. **The Ring** — Discovery in the desk
2. **The Inscription** — Her name, impossible date
3. **Michael's Denial** — His reaction, her doubt
4. **The Investigation** — Finding the records
5. **The Photographs** — A woman who looks like her
6. **The Marriage** — Evidence of a previous wedding
7. **The Questions** — What does Michael actually know?
8. **Finding the Other** — Tracking the first wife
9. **The Truth About Michael** — What he's been hiding
10. **The Other Wife** — Who she really was
11. **The Other Wife** — Resolution, revelation
