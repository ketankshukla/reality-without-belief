# Everything She Never Said

## Author
**Vera Sinclair** — Psychological Thriller

---

## Genre
Psychological Thriller / Domestic Suspense / Marriage Thriller

---

## Concept

When Mara Thorne's husband confesses to an affair, she thinks she knows what's coming. The tears, the therapy, the slow work of rebuilding trust. She's seen friends go through it. She's prepared for the devastation.

What she's not prepared for is the relief she feels.

Because David's confession gives her permission to look. To dig through emails, bank statements, phone records. To finally investigate the small inconsistencies she's been ignoring for years. She tells herself she's just confirming the affair, looking for the details she needs to decide whether to stay or go.

But what she finds isn't another woman. It's evidence of something else entirely — something David confessed to the affair to distract her from. The affair was real, but it was also a controlled demolition: better she discover that than what he's actually been doing.

Mara's husband has a secret life. One that's been running parallel to their marriage for over a decade. And the deeper she digs, the more she realizes: she's not the only one who's been looking. Someone else knows what David is. And they've been watching Mara too, waiting to see how much she'll uncover.

## Key Themes
- What we choose not to see in our partners
- Confession as misdirection
- The architecture of hidden lives
- When knowing becomes dangerous

## Tone
Domestic noir. The investigation of a marriage reveals something worse than infidelity.

## Unique Angle
The affair confession is the misdirection. The real secret is darker.

---

## Target Audience
Readers who love marriage thrillers, domestic suspense, reveals that compound.

## Suggested Chapter Structure (11 chapters)

1. **The Confession** — David admits the affair
2. **Permission** — Mara begins to investigate
3. **The Trail** — Finding more than expected
4. **Inconsistencies** — The affair doesn't explain everything
5. **The Other Life** — Discovering what he's really been doing
6. **The Watcher** — Realizing someone else knows
7. **Deeper** — How long has this been happening?
8. **Confrontation** — Challenging David
9. **The Trap** — Understanding why he confessed when he did
10. **The Truth** — What it all means
11. **Everything She Never Said** — Resolution
