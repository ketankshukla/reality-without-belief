# The Last City

## Author
**Nova Sinclair** — Dystopian Fiction

---

## Genre
Dystopian / Post-Apocalyptic / Siege Narrative

---

## Concept

New Geneva is the last functioning city in North America — or so its citizens believe. Protected by walls, powered by geothermal energy, governed by the Council of Founders. Five million people in the only place that works.

Outside the walls: the Wastes. Radiation, climate collapse, roving bands of survivors who've devolved into something barely human. That's what the Council says. That's why no one leaves. That's why everyone is grateful for their place inside.

Dr. Amira Okonkwo works in City Planning, optimizing resource allocation for a population that grows while resources don't. She's been noticing discrepancies: more people entering the city than the records show, supplies arriving from sources that shouldn't exist, Council members traveling outside and returning unchanged.

When she investigates, she discovers the truth: there are other cities. At least twelve functioning settlements across the continent, all believing they're alone, all controlled by branches of the same Council. The isolation is artificial. The fear is manufactured. The scarcity is a choice.

The Council isn't saving the last city. They're running a continent-wide experiment in controlled populations. And Amira has found the communications that prove it.
