# The Wall Keepers

## Author
**Nova Sinclair** — Dystopian Fiction

---

## Genre
Dystopian / Border Drama / Moral Thriller

---

## Concept

The Wall separates the Safe Zone from the Contaminated Territories. Three hundred miles of concrete, steel, and automated defenses, maintained by the Wall Keepers — soldiers who patrol the barrier and ensure no one crosses. In either direction.

Corporal Dani Reyes has served on the Wall for four years. She's seen the desperate people on the other side, watched them try to climb, dig under, break through. She's followed orders. She's done what the Safe Zone requires to stay safe.

Then she's assigned to a new section — and discovers what happens to the people who actually make it over. They're not processed for entry. They're not tested for contamination. They disappear into facilities that officially don't exist, and the soldiers who ask questions disappear too.

Dani starts investigating and finds the truth: the Contaminated Territories aren't contaminated anymore. The danger passed years ago. But the Safe Zone's economy depends on fear of the outside. The wall doesn't keep danger out — it keeps cheap labor in. The crossers aren't being protected from contamination. They're being processed into an invisible workforce.

Dani has to decide: stay quiet and keep her position, or expose a system that her entire society depends on.
