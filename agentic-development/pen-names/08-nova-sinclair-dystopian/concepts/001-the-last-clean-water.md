# The Last Clean Water

## Author
**Nova Sinclair** — Dystopian Fiction

---

## Genre
Dystopian / Post-Apocalyptic / Survival Fiction

---

## Concept

Twenty years after the Contamination, clean water is worth more than gold. The aquifers are poisoned, the rivers run toxic, and the few remaining purification plants are controlled by the Water Barons — corporate warlords who trade survival for servitude.

Elena Torres works as a water scout for Baron Reyes, mapping underground sources in the wastes between settlements. It's dangerous work — raiders, contamination zones, the desperate people who'll kill for a single clean gallon — but it keeps her family supplied and protected.

Then she finds something that changes everything: an untouched aquifer beneath the Nevada desert. Clean water. Enough for ten thousand people for a hundred years. The find that could break the Barons' control forever.

Elena knows what happens to scouts who find major sources. They disappear. Their families disappear. The water becomes the Baron's property. But she also knows what happens to settlements that can't pay the Barons' prices. She's watched people die of thirst while tankers full of water drove past.

She has a choice: report the find and trust the Baron, or somehow get this information to the people who need it most — the Free Settlements who've been fighting the Barons for two decades.
