# The Skin Collector

## Author
**Silas Crow** — Supernatural Horror

---

## Genre
Supernatural Horror / Cosmic Horror / Body Horror

---

## Concept

The bodies are perfect. That's what makes Detective Sarah Vance's case so disturbing. Seven victims in three months, all found without a mark on them — no wounds, no bruising, no signs of violence. The coroner rules each death "natural causes," but natural causes don't explain why healthy people in their twenties and thirties simply stop living.

Then Sarah sees the photographs from the scenes and notices what everyone else missed: the skin. Each victim's skin is subtly wrong. Too smooth. Too uniform. As if it were placed there rather than grown.

The victims weren't killed. They were collected.

Something is taking people — not their lives, but their exteriors. It leaves behind perfect replicas, empty shells that fool everyone except the one detective paranoid enough to look closer. And it's been doing this for longer than anyone realizes. Sarah finds records going back decades, centuries if she believes the oldest accounts. An entity that covets human appearance, that wears collected skins like costumes, that walks among us in faces that belonged to the dead.

It's noticed her investigation. And it's decided she has exactly the face it wants next.
