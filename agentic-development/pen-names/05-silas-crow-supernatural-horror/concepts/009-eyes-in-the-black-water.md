# Eyes in the Black Water

## Author
**Silas Crow** — Supernatural Horror

---

## Genre
Supernatural Horror / Deep Water Horror / Lovecraftian

---

## Concept

Lake Morrow has no recorded bottom. Sonar returns impossible readings — depths that exceed the geological formations of the area, echoes that suggest vast open spaces where solid rock should be. The locals have always known to stay away from the water after dark. They don't remember why anymore, only that they do.

When a research team arrives to map the lake's anomalies, Dr. Elena Voss leads the dive that changes everything. What they find at 400 meters isn't a lake bottom — it's an opening. A passage into somewhere else.

They send down cameras first. The footage shows structures — not natural formations, but architecture. Carved stone, massive in scale, built by something that understood engineering. And in the structures, movement. Eyes reflecting the camera lights. Many, many eyes.

The research team plans to leave, to report their findings and let someone else deal with the implications. But the lake has other plans. Since the dive, the water level has been rising. Slowly, imperceptibly, but measurably. Whatever they woke up is coming closer to the surface. And the eyes that watched the cameras are now watching the shore.
