# What Sleeps Beneath Ravenscroft

## Author
**Silas Crow** — Supernatural Horror

---

## Genre
Supernatural Horror / Gothic Horror / Family Curse

---

## Concept

The Blackwood family has owned Ravenscroft Manor for two hundred years. They've also been dying there, generation after generation, in ways that are never quite explicable. Heart attacks in healthy thirty-year-olds. Falls from stairs that physics doesn't explain. Drownings in inches of water.

When Claire Blackwood inherits the estate, she's the last of her line — and she's determined to understand why.

The answer is in the foundation.

When her ancestor built Ravenscroft, he built it on top of something. Not a cemetery or a sacred ground — something alive. Something that had been sleeping for longer than human memory, and that woke when the first foundation stone was laid. It's been feeding on Blackwoods ever since. Slowly. Patiently. Taking one per generation, always the heir, always in ways that look like accidents.

Claire is next.

But Claire is different. She's the first Blackwood who's been told the truth — by a great-aunt everyone thought was mad, whose warnings were dismissed as dementia. Claire knows what's coming. She has the journals that document every death. And she's determined to end the cycle, even if ending it means destroying everything her family built.

## Key Themes
- Family curses and complicity
- What we build on without knowing
- The mad relatives who were right
- Breaking cycles that span generations

---

## Tone
Gothic, ancestral dread, slow revelation of deep history.

---

## Unique Angle
The protagonist knows from the start. Her challenge is stopping it, not discovering it.

---

## Target Audience
Gothic horror fans, family curse narratives, readers who love ancestral mysteries.

## Suggested Chapter Structure (11 chapters)

1. **The Inheritance** — Claire arrives, the warnings
2. **The Journals** — Reading her great-aunt's records
3. **The History** — Two hundred years of deaths
4. **The Foundation** — What the house was built on
5. **Research** — Understanding what sleeps there
6. **First Encounter** — It knows she knows
7. **The Pattern** — How it chooses, how it takes
8. **Attempted Solutions** — Trying to stop it
9. **The Bargain** — What it wants
10. **The Destruction** — What Claire decides
11. **What Sleeps Beneath** — Resolution
