# The Demon Broker

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Demon Politics / Supernatural Business

---

## Concept

Samira Reyes doesn't summon demons. She negotiates with them. As a demon broker, she facilitates contracts between humans who want something and demons who can provide it — for a price. She ensures both parties understand the terms, witnesses the agreements, and takes a percentage for her services.

It's a lucrative business, and Samira is very good at it. She's built a reputation for fair dealing in a profession where fair dealing is rare. Demons respect her because she knows their laws better than they do. Humans trust her because she's never let a client sign a contract they didn't understand.

Then a client comes to her with an unusual request: he wants to break a contract that was signed forty years ago. By his mother. A contract that traded his soul before he was born.

The demon who holds the contract is one of the most powerful in the hierarchy — and one who's never had a deal broken. Samira would normally refuse. But the client is dying, and the clause that will trigger on his death will give the demon access to something that could tip the balance between all the powers in the city.

Samira has to find a loophole in a perfect contract. And the demon is watching her try.

## Suggested Chapter Structure (10 chapters)

1. **The Business** — Samira's work, a typical deal
2. **The Client** — The impossible request
3. **The Contract** — Examining the terms
4. **The Stakes** — What happens if he dies
5. **The Demon** — Understanding the opponent
6. **Research** — Looking for loopholes
7. **The Negotiation** — Approaching the demon
8. **The Trap** — The demon's countermove
9. **The Loophole** — Finding the way out
10. **The Demon Broker** — Resolution
