# Necromancer for Hire

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Supernatural Detective / Dark Comedy

---

## Concept

Ethan Cross talks to dead people. Not in a "I see dead people" way — more like "I raise them temporarily to ask questions and then put them back." As the only licensed necromancer in Seattle, he consults for police departments, law firms, and anyone willing to pay his rates. Cause of death, last words, hidden assets — the dead know everything they knew in life, and Ethan knows how to ask.

His latest client is the widow of a billionaire tech CEO who wants to know where her husband hid $50 million before his "accidental" death. Simple job: raise the corpse, ask the question, collect the fee.

Except the corpse doesn't want to answer. The dead man is hiding something, and he's more afraid of Ethan finding it than he is of eternal rest. When Ethan pushes harder, the corpse tries to kill him — a feat that should be impossible for a properly raised spirit.

Someone else got to the body first. Someone who bound the dead man's soul with magic older and darker than necromancy. And whatever the CEO was hiding, it's worth killing (or un-killing) for.
