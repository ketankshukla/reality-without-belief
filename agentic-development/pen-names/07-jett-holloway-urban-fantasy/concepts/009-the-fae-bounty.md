# The Fae Bounty

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Supernatural Bounty Hunter / Dark Fantasy

---

## Concept

When fae break mortal law, human courts can't touch them. When they break fae law in mortal territory, their own courts won't bother. That's where Zara Cole comes in. She's a licensed fae bounty hunter — one of the few humans authorized by both courts to track down and retrieve fae who've crossed lines.

Her latest target is a sidhe lord named Caelan who's been preying on humans in Portland. The fae courts want him back for trial. The human authorities want the killings stopped. The bounty is the largest Zara's ever seen.

She finds Caelan easily — too easily. He's not running. He's waiting. And he has a proposition: the fae who sent her to capture him are the real predators. He has evidence. He'll come quietly if she helps him expose them first.

Zara doesn't trust fae. But the evidence Caelan shows her is damning, and the lords who hired her are powerful enough to bury anyone who asks questions. If she brings Caelan in, she's delivering an innocent to execution. If she helps him, she becomes a target of the most dangerous beings in both worlds.
