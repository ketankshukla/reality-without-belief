# The Last Exorcist

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Supernatural Action / Religious Horror

---

## Concept

Father Daniel Vance was defrocked five years ago for performing an unauthorized exorcism that killed the possessed. The Church said he was reckless. Daniel knows he saved a soul from something that had already consumed the body. He's been performing exorcisms outside Church sanction ever since — freelance, expensive, and effective.

When a tech billionaire's daughter shows signs of possession, Daniel is offered enough money to disappear forever. But this possession is different. The entity inside Sarah Chen isn't a demon — it's something older, something that predates Christian mythology, something that the Church's rites were never designed to address.

Daniel needs help from sources he's spent his career avoiding: the supernatural community that exists in the shadows of Seattle. Practitioners, spirits, entities who have their own reasons for wanting this thing gone. Working with them means owing favors to things he was trained to destroy.

The entity in Sarah is getting stronger. It's also getting interested in Daniel — specifically, in the question of why a faithless priest still has the power to drive out darkness. The answer might save the girl. It might also destroy everything Daniel believes about where his power comes from.
