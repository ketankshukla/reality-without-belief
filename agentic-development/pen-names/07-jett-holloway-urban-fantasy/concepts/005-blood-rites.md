# Blood Rites

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Vampire Politics / Supernatural Action

---

## Concept

In Boston, the vampire courts have kept peace for two centuries through careful diplomacy, arranged marriages, and the occasional discreet assassination. Detective Ray Santos has worked their periphery for years — a human cop who knows enough about the supernatural to be useful, not enough to be dangerous.

Then someone starts killing vampires.

Not in the usual ways — sunlight, stakes, the methods vampires know to defend against. These deaths are different. Vampires are aging centuries in seconds, collapsing into dust that was old before Columbus. Whatever's doing this is using magic that shouldn't exist anymore — magic the vampires thought they'd eliminated along with everyone who knew it.

The vampire courts want Ray to investigate. He's human, which means he can go places they can't. He's also expendable, which means they don't care if the killer gets him too.

Ray takes the case because the alternative is having his memory wiped. What he discovers is that the killer isn't an enemy of vampires — they're a vampire. One old enough to remember when their kind first made the bargain that gave them immortality. One who's decided the debt has come due.
