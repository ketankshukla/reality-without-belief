# Witch Hunter for Hire

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Supernatural Action / Detective Fantasy

---

## Concept

Cassandra "Cass" Vega is a witch hunter — one of the last trained by the old traditions. She tracks down practitioners who've broken the Accords, the ancient agreements that keep the magical and mundane worlds from colliding. Rogue witches, blood mages, summoners who've lost control of what they called up. She finds them. She stops them. She doesn't ask questions about what "stop" means.

Then the Coven Council hires her to hunt one of their own: Elara Blackwood, High Witch of the Eastern Seaboard, who's allegedly gone rogue and started practicing forbidden magic. The bounty is enough to retire on. The target is one of the most powerful witches alive.

Cass takes the job. What she finds isn't a rogue witch — it's a woman who discovered something the Coven Council wants buried. Something about where witch power actually comes from, and why the Accords were really created. The Council didn't hire Cass to stop a criminal. They hired her to silence a whistleblower.

Now Cass has a choice: complete the contract and stay alive, or protect the target and make enemies of every major magical power in North America.

---

## Key Themes
- Professional ethics in supernatural work
- Questioning the systems you serve
- Power structures and their secrets
- The hunter becoming the hunted

---

## Tone
Hard-boiled urban fantasy, moral complexity, action-driven.

---

## Unique Angle
Witch hunter hired by witches to kill a witch. The job is a cover-up.

---

## Target Audience
Urban fantasy fans who like competent protagonists and conspiracy plots.
