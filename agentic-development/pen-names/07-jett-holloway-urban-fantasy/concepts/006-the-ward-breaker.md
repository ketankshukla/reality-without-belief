# The Ward Breaker

## Author
**Jett Holloway** — Urban Fantasy

---

## Genre
Urban Fantasy / Magical Security / Supernatural Heist

---

## Concept

Maya Chen breaks wards for a living. Magical protections, supernatural barriers, curses designed to keep people out — she disassembles them all. In a city where the wealthy protect their secrets with magic, someone needs to get through when locks aren't enough.

Her latest client wants into a penthouse protected by wards so sophisticated they could only have been created by the Architect — a legendary wardsmith who disappeared thirty years ago. The client claims there's evidence inside that will expose a conspiracy. The payment is enough to set Maya up for life.

The job should be straightforward. Analyze the wards, find the weaknesses, break through. But these wards aren't just protection — they're a puzzle. A message. The Architect designed them to be broken by someone with exactly Maya's skills, and he designed them thirty years before she was born.

Inside the penthouse, Maya finds what the Architect wanted her to find: proof that the magical establishment has been lying about the source of their power for generations. The conspiracy is real. And now that she knows, the same people who silenced the Architect are coming for her.

---
