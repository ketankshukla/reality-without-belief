# Ghost Protocol: Andromeda

## Author
**Cole Harker** — Military Sci-Fi

---

## Genre
Military Science Fiction / Special Operations / Thriller

---

## Concept

Specter Team doesn't exist. That's the official position of the United Earth Navy, and it's technically true — there are no records, no budget lines, no personnel files. Just twelve operatives who go where conventional forces can't and do what conventional forces won't.

Captain Maya Torres leads Specter into the Andromeda Sector on what should be a standard extraction: retrieve a deep-cover intelligence asset before enemy counterintel finds him. Forty-eight hours, minimal footprint, no complications.

They find the asset. They also find something else.

Hidden in an asteroid mining facility is a weapons program the enemy has kept off every intelligence brief. Not ships, not soldiers — something biological. Something that turns human beings into weapons. The asset has proof, but the proof is in his head, and he's too damaged to extract conventionally.

Now Specter Team is trapped behind enemy lines with a broken asset, evidence of atrocities that could change the war, and an enemy that knows someone has discovered their secret. The extraction window is closed. Reinforcement is impossible. And the enemy is deploying the very weapons they came to expose — against them.

This is not a story about winning. It's a story about getting out with something worth dying for.

## Target Audience
Military thriller readers, special operations enthusiasts, fans of tense extraction narratives.

---

## Suggested Chapter Structure (11 chapters)

1. **The Brief** — Mission parameters, team assembly
2. **Insertion** — Getting into enemy territory
3. **The Asset** — Finding him, assessing the damage
4. **The Discovery** — What else is in this facility
5. **Compromised** — The enemy knows they're there
6. **The Hunters** — Being tracked by what they found
7. **Scattered** — Team separated, regrouping
8. **The Evidence** — What they need to extract, why it matters
9. **The Gauntlet** — Fighting to exfil point
10. **The Choice** — What can be saved, what must be sacrificed
11. **Ghost Protocol** — Escape or last stand
