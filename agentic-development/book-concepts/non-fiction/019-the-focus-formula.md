# The Focus Formula

## Genre
Productivity / Psychology / Self-Development

## Concept

Focus has become the scarcest resource in the modern economy. The ability to concentrate deeply on a single task for extended periods is now so rare that it's a genuine competitive advantage. This book provides a systematic approach to reclaiming and strengthening your ability to focus.

Drawing on neuroscience, behavioral psychology, and practical case studies, this book covers: why focus has become so difficult, how attention actually works in the brain, environmental design for concentration, techniques for entering and maintaining deep focus, and recovery practices that restore attentional capacity.

The focus formula isn't a single trick — it's a comprehensive system for building and protecting your most valuable cognitive resource.

## Key Themes

- The neuroscience of attention
