# Image Prompt Templates

## Overview

This document provides reusable templates for generating high-quality image prompts. These templates are derived from the Reality Without Belief project and can be adapted for any book project.

**Tools for Image Generation:**
- ChatGPT (GPT-4o with image generation)
- Flux Pro (via Flow or API)
- Midjourney
- Ideogram 2.0

---

## Template 1: Book Cover (9:16 Portrait)

### Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 2700 × 4800 pixels (9:16 portrait)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Style:** Photorealistic, cinematic

### Template Structure

```
A stunning 9:16 portrait book cover rendered in ultra-high-resolution photorealistic style with cinematic color grading.

SUBJECT — [SUBJECT TYPE]:
[Detailed description of the main subject — person, scene, or symbolic element. Include specific details about appearance, expression, pose, lighting on subject, and emotional quality. If human figure: describe age, ethnicity, gender, expression, clothing, positioning in frame.]

CRITICAL COMPOSITION RULE — [ELEMENT] PLACEMENT:
[Specific instructions for where key elements must be positioned to avoid text overlap. The subject should be positioned entirely BELOW the title/subtitle text area and ABOVE the author name.]

BACKGROUND — [THEMATIC DESCRIPTION]:
[Detailed description of background elements that support the book's themes. Include symbolic imagery, lighting effects, depth, atmosphere, and how background relates to foreground subject.]

TEXT — MUST BE RENDERED EXACTLY AS SPECIFIED:

All text placement percentages are measured from the TOP of the image.

Line 1 (exactly [X]% from top): "[SERIES NAME]" — [font], [size], [color], [letter-spacing].

Line 2 (exactly [X]% from top): "BOOK [N]" — [font], [size], [color], [letter-spacing]. [Optional: decorative elements].

Lines 3-4 (title block, exactly [X-X]% from top): "[TITLE]" — [font], [size], [color]. [Line breaks and emphasis notes].

Decorative ornamental line (exactly [X]% from top): [Description of decorative element].

Line 5 (exactly [X]% from top): "[Subtitle]" — [font], [size], [color].

Line 6 (exactly [X]% from top): "[AUTHOR NAME]" — [font], [size], [color], [letter-spacing].

OVERALL MOOD: [2-3 sentence description of the emotional impact, visual style, and target aesthetic. Include what the cover should communicate at thumbnail size and full resolution.]

PHOTOREALISTIC CINEMATIC DIGITAL ART. NO oil painting. NO watercolor. NO traditional media.
```

## Template 2: Book Cover with Human Figure

### Example (Based on Reality Without Belief)

```
A stunning 9:16 portrait book cover rendered in ultra-high-resolution photorealistic style with cinematic color grading.

SUBJECT — CLOSE-UP PORTRAIT (WOMAN):
A breathtakingly beautiful young white woman in her 20s, positioned in the lower-right quadrant of the image (her face centered around 65-75% down from the top, offset slightly right of center). She is captured in a close-up three-quarter portrait, her face large and dominant, filling the lower-right portion of the frame. She is looking upward and slightly to the left with wide, illuminated eyes — the expression of someone experiencing [EMOTIONAL MOMENT RELEVANT TO BOOK THEME]. Her face radiates [EMOTION] — not [OPPOSITE], but the quiet [QUALITY] of [THEMATIC DESCRIPTION]. Her right hand is raised slightly beside her face, fingers gently spread, as if she has just [SYMBOLIC GESTURE RELEVANT TO THEME].

Her face is captured with hyper-real detail — flawless yet entirely natural skin with visible pores across her nose and cheeks, a scattering of faint freckles across the bridge of her nose, the faintest hint of peach fuzz along her jawline caught by the light. Her eyebrows are full, naturally shaped, with individual hairs visible. Her eyes are wide open and luminous — large, striking [COLOR] irises with [DETAIL] striations radiating outward from the pupil. Natural catchlights — two sharp, luminous reflections — sit in the upper portion of each iris. Her lashes are thick and dark, naturally fanned. Her lips are soft, naturally [COLOR], parted in a gentle [EXPRESSION]. She is stunningly gorgeous yet radiates simplicity and authenticity — no vanity, only quiet strength and [QUALITY RELEVANT TO BOOK].

CRITICAL COMPOSITION RULE — FACE PLACEMENT:
The woman's face must be positioned entirely BELOW the subtitle text area and ABOVE the author name. Her face occupies roughly the 55%-82% vertical zone of the image. The top 45% of the image (where the title and subtitle text will be placed) must contain ONLY the background elements and open space — absolutely NO part of her face, hair, or hand may enter the top 35% of the image.

BACKGROUND — [THEMATIC TITLE]:
Behind her and slightly out of focus, the background depicts [SYMBOLIC SCENE RELEVANT TO BOOK THEMES]. [Detailed description of background elements — objects, lighting, atmosphere, symbolic imagery]. Volumetric [COLOR] light rays stream through [ELEMENT], creating dramatic god-rays against the [COLOR] background. Tiny [COLOR] particles and luminous dust motes float throughout the entire scene.

TEXT — MUST BE RENDERED EXACTLY AS SPECIFIED:

Line 1 (exactly 3% from top): "[SERIES NAME]" — small elegant Garamond font, centered horizontally, [color] (#[HEX]), letter-spacing 0.15em, approximately 14pt equivalent.

Line 2 (exactly 6% from top): "BOOK [N]" — Garamond font, centered, [color], letter-spacing 0.2em, approximately 12pt equivalent. Flanked on the left and right by thin horizontal [color] lines extending outward from the text.

Lines 3-4 (title block, exactly 10-22% from top): "[TITLE]" — large, elegant Garamond font, centered, white/cream with subtle [color] warmth. [Arrangement notes for multi-word titles].

Decorative ornamental line (exactly 24% from top): An elegant [color] ornamental filigree divider — a thin horizontal line that blooms into delicate curving scrollwork and flourishes at the center.

Line 5 (exactly 26% from top): "[Subtitle]" — smaller italic Garamond, centered, light gray/cream color.

Decorative ornamental line (exactly 28% from top): An identical [color] ornamental filigree divider matching the one above.

Line 6 (exactly 92% from top): "[AUTHOR NAME]" — large Garamond font, centered, white/cream, generous letter-spacing approximately 0.2em.

OVERALL MOOD: Cinematic, [EMOTION], [COLOR] tones against [BACKGROUND COLOR]. The feeling of [EMOTIONAL DESCRIPTION RELEVANT TO BOOK]. Photorealistic, editorial quality, suitable for a bestselling [GENRE] book cover.
```

## Template 3: Series Logo

### Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1200 × 1200 pixels (square)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Style:** Clean, iconic, scalable

### Template Structure

```
A sophisticated, iconic logo design for the "[SERIES NAME]" book series.

CONCEPT:
[Description of the core visual concept that captures the series' essence]

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Primary symbol: [Description of main iconic element]
- Typography: [Font style and arrangement]
- Negative space: [How negative space is used meaningfully]

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: [Color with hex code]
- Secondary: [Color with hex code]
- Accent: [Color with hex code]

COMPOSITION:
- The symbol is [position] relative to the text
- Text reads "[SERIES NAME]" in [font style]
- The overall shape fits within [square/circular/other] bounds
- Must be recognizable at small sizes (favicon, social media avatar)

STYLE:
Clean, modern, professional. Must work in:
- Full color
- Single color (black or white)
- Reversed on dark backgrounds

NO gradients that won't print well. NO overly detailed elements that disappear at small sizes.
```

## Template 4: Box Set Cover (Portrait)

### Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 2700 × 4800 pixels (9:16 portrait) or custom
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Style:** Unified with individual book covers

### Template Structure

```
A breathtaking ultra-high-resolution box set cover for "[SERIES NAME]" — [NUMBER] books in one stunning image.

CONCEPT — THE COMPLETE JOURNEY:
[Description of how this cover visually represents the entire series arc, showing progression or transformation across all books]

VISUAL STRUCTURE:
[How the image is divided to represent multiple books — visual zones, transitions, or unified scene]

CENTRAL FIGURE (if applicable):
[Description of main figure that bridges all books — represents the reader's journey]

BACKGROUND ELEMENTS:
[Left section representing early books]
[Center section representing middle books]  
[Right section representing later books]
[How these sections transition and connect]

CRITICAL COMPOSITION RULE:
The image is divided into strict zones with NO overlap:
- ZONE 1 — TEXT BLOCK (top 0-18%): ALL text except author name. NO characters or key elements.
- ZONE 2 — CHARACTER ZONE (25-88%): Central figure and all symbolic elements.
- ZONE 3 — AUTHOR NAME (90-96%): ONLY the author name.

TEXT — RENDERED EXACTLY AS SPECIFIED:

Line 1 (3% from top): "[SERIES NAME]" — [font], [size], [color]
Line 2 (6% from top): "THE COMPLETE SERIES" — [font], gold color
Line 3 (9% from top): "ALL [NUMBER] BOOKS" — [font], [size], [color]
Decorative line (12% from top): [Description incorporating motifs from all books]
Line 4 (14% from top): "[Book themes separated by |]" — [font], [size]
Line 5 (17% from top): "[Tagline]" — italic, [color]
Line 6 (93% from top): "[AUTHOR NAME]" — [font], [size], [color]

OVERALL MOOD: [Description of the emotional impact and what the cover communicates about owning the complete series]

PHOTOREALISTIC CINEMATIC DIGITAL ART.
```

## Template 5: Social Media Banner (Landscape)

### Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 2400 × 1350 pixels (16:9) or 1500 × 500 (Twitter header)
- **Resolution:** 150-300 DPI
- **Style:** Consistent with book covers

### Template Structure

```
A stunning landscape promotional banner for "[BOOK/SERIES TITLE]" — [DIMENSIONS].

COMPOSITION:
[Description of horizontal layout — what's on left, center, right]

FOCAL POINT:
[Main visual element that draws the eye — typically positioned using rule of thirds]

BACKGROUND:
[Atmospheric elements that support the book's themes without overwhelming text]

TEXT ZONE (must be clearly readable):
Position: [Left third / Center / Right third]
- Title: "[TITLE]" — [font], prominent size
- Subtitle: "[Subtitle]" — [font], secondary size
- Author: "[AUTHOR]" — [font]
- Tagline: "[Call to action or key phrase]"

NEGATIVE SPACE:
[Where text will be placed — this area must remain uncluttered]

COLOR PALETTE:
Consistent with book cover — [primary color], [secondary color], [accent]

MOOD: [Brief description of emotional impact]

Must work at:
- Full resolution (digital ads)
- Compressed sizes (social feeds)
- With and without text overlay options
```

## Template 6: YouTube Thumbnail

### Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1920 × 1080 pixels (16:9)
- **Resolution:** 150 DPI
- **Style:** High contrast, attention-grabbing

### Template Structure

```
A compelling YouTube thumbnail for "[VIDEO TITLE]" — book trailer / author interview / chapter summary.

COMPOSITION:
The thumbnail must be readable at 320×180 pixels (small thumbnail size).

PRIMARY ELEMENT:
[Main visual — face with expression, book cover, or key symbolic image]
Position: [Typically right 2/3 or center]
Scale: [Large enough to be recognizable at thumbnail size]

TEXT OVERLAY:
Position: [Typically left 1/3 or top]
Content: [2-4 words maximum]
Style: Bold, high contrast, with subtle shadow or outline for legibility

BACKGROUND:
[Solid color, gradient, or simple blurred imagery]
Must not compete with primary element or text

COLOR STRATEGY:
- Use contrasting colors (complementary on color wheel)
- Avoid YouTube red (blends with UI)
- High saturation for attention

EXPRESSION (if face):
[Strong emotion — surprise, confidence, intensity, joy]
Eyes visible and engaging camera

REQUIRED ELEMENTS:
- High contrast for visibility
- Clear focal point
- Maximum 4 words of text
- Bold, simple composition
```

## Template 7: Author Photo Prompt

### Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1200 × 1500 pixels (4:5) or 1200 × 1200 (square)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Style:** Professional, approachable

### Template Structure

```
A professional author headshot photograph.

SUBJECT:
[Gender], [age range], [ethnicity], [general appearance notes]

EXPRESSION:
[Warm and approachable / Confident and authoritative / Thoughtful and introspective]
Direct eye contact with camera
Slight smile or neutral expression

POSE:
Head and shoulders composition
[Facing camera directly / Slight 3/4 turn]
[Arms crossed / Hands relaxed / One hand near face]

CLOTHING:
[Professional casual / Business formal / Genre-appropriate]
Solid colors work best — avoid busy patterns
Colors that complement skin tone and background

LIGHTING:
Professional studio lighting with:
- Soft key light from [angle]
- Fill light to reduce harsh shadows
- Subtle rim/hair light for separation from background

BACKGROUND:
[Solid neutral color / Gradient / Blurred office/library/outdoor scene]
Should not distract from subject

MOOD:
This is a [GENRE] author who readers should see as:
[Trustworthy / Expert / Relatable / Inspiring / Thoughtful]

TECHNICAL:
Sharp focus on eyes
Shallow depth of field for background separation
Natural skin tones — not over-retouched
```

## Color Palette Reference

### From Reality Without Belief Project

| Element | Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|---------|-------|----------|-------|
| Primary | Deep Turquoise | #00CED1 | Accents, decorative elements |
| Background | Deep Black | #0A0A0A | Cover backgrounds |
| Text Primary | White/Cream | #FFFEF0 | Titles, author name |
| Text Secondary | Light Gray | #C0C0C0 | Subtitles |
| Warm Accent | Amber Gold | #FFD700 | Box set, premium elements |

### Suggested Palettes by Genre

**Self-Help / Personal Development:**
- Primary: Warm gold or sunrise orange
- Secondary: Deep navy or charcoal
- Accent: White or cream

**Philosophy / Spirituality:**
- Primary: Deep purple or indigo
- Secondary: Silver or white
- Accent: Gold

**Business / Professional:**
- Primary: Navy blue or forest green
- Secondary: White or light gray
- Accent: Gold or orange

**Fiction — Thriller:**
- Primary: Black or dark red
- Secondary: Silver or white
- Accent: Red or gold

**Fiction — Romance:**
- Primary: Deep red or pink
- Secondary: Cream or gold
- Accent: Rose gold

**Fiction — Fantasy:**
- Primary: Deep purple or teal
- Secondary: Gold or silver
- Accent: Magical blue or green

## Typography Reference

### Recommended Font Pairings

**Classic/Literary:**
- Title: Garamond Bold
- Subtitle: Garamond Italic
- Author: Garamond Regular with wide tracking

**Modern/Clean:**
- Title: Montserrat Bold
- Subtitle: Montserrat Light
- Author: Montserrat Medium

**Elegant/Premium:**
- Title: Playfair Display Bold
- Subtitle: Lato Light Italic
- Author: Playfair Display Regular

### Text Specifications

| Element | Size (relative) | Weight | Spacing |
|---------|----------------|--------|---------|
| Series Name | Small (14pt equiv) | Regular | 0.15em |
| Book Number | Small (12pt equiv) | Regular | 0.2em |
| Title | Large (varies) | Bold | Standard |
| Subtitle | Medium (64pt equiv) | Italic | Standard |
| Author Name | Large (120pt equiv) | Bold | 0.2-0.25em |

## Prompt Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any image prompt, verify:

- [ ] Dimensions and resolution specified
- [ ] Color space mentioned (sRGB for digital)
- [ ] Style clearly defined (photorealistic, illustrated, etc.)
- [ ] Composition rules for text placement included
- [ ] Specific percentages for text positioning
- [ ] All text content specified exactly as it should appear
- [ ] Font names, sizes, and colors specified
- [ ] Background described in detail
- [ ] Mood/emotional impact described
- [ ] Technical requirements stated (no oil painting, etc.)
- [ ] Works at thumbnail and full resolution
- [ ] Consistent with series visual identity (if applicable)
